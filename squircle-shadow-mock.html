Takeaway: if you squircle every rounded element (the skill's universal mode), every box-shadow on a rounded element gets clipped. To keep shadows you either (a) swap box-shadow → filter: drop-shadow() on those elements, or (b) add data-no-squircle so they keep native radius + box-shadow. The middle column is what you'd silently ship without doing either.
